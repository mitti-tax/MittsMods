Hi, I'm Dimitri - aka Mitt.

I'm a Perth-based IT student and console modder with a passion for fixing broken and discarded gaming hardware. I started seriously modding in June 2024, though the curiosity goes back to 2015 when I got my first laptop.

MittsMods is where I share my fixes, builds, and lessons. I'm not here to write perfect guides, but to keep a journal of information that might help someone out.
